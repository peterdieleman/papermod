## Rules

- Vowels that are followed directly by **ß** are  'long', e.g. **Fuß**
- Vowels that are followed directly by **ck** or **ch** are 'short', e.g. **noch**, **Wecker**
- Difference between **ß** and **ss**:
  - **Buße**, pronounced with long u
  - **Busse**, plural of bus
