The Dutch equivalent of 'Inuits have 50 words for snow'.
All of these refer to artificial bodies of water that are longer than they are wide.

- Delf
- Diep
- Gracht
  - Stadsgracht
  - Slotgracht
  - Kasteelgracht
- Greppel
- Grift
- Kanaal
- Plomp(erd)
- Singel
- Sloot
- Tocht
- Vaart
  - Trekvaart
  - Ringvaart
- Vest
- Vliet
- Watergang
- Watering
- Waterloop
- Wetering
- Waterweg
- Zwet
